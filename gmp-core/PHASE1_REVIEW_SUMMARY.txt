PHASE 2 REQUIREMENTS (NOT YET IMPLEMENTED)
===========================================

1. Key Rotation - No mechanism for rotating compromised signing keys
2. Nonce Tracking - No persistence of nonce values across connections
3. Rate Limiting - No protection against connection flood attacks
4. Clock Dependency - Timestamp checks vulnerable to clock manipulation
5. Peer Reputation - No mechanism to handle misbehaving peers
6. NAT Traversal - Direct reachability required (TCP hole punching defer
red)
7. Multi-Hop Routing - No A->B->C path establishment
8. Address Truncation - Full 64-byte NodeID used (Phase 1.5 feature)
